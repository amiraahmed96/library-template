About

Library.com

library is a community of more than half a million authors and readers. library helps authors publish and distribute their eBooks (and the service is free) and allow members to discuss their work and get feedback from each other. The massive Web 2.0 – Projects, which have been hugely popular with music, video and photography fans, now have a sister platform, which will delight literature fans around the world: library. library provides an online destination where authors can showcase their work. Registered users will get to design their own personal profile page. This gives users the opportunity to create their own books, recommend their favorite literature and promote themselves as authors and/or readers. Once your book is complete, you can use BookRix FREE Publishing Service to distribute your book(s) to all the major eBook stores (Amazon, Kobo, Barnes & Noble, iTunes, etc.). Learn more – library eBook Self-Publishing Service! If you have any questions, contact us at support-en@library.com

library provides users with a platform to:

  • find other readers and authors
  • share thoughts about many interests in groups
  • discuss books and projects
  • join author fan clubs
  • participate in writing competitions with like-minded people in a big literary network
  • write reviews
  • send your books to friends and family via e-mail
  • find top-rated books and all new works
  • self-publish your book for free and distribute it to all the major eBook stores
